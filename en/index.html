Your Private Jungle Escape in Hengchun

Looking for a Full House Rental in Kenting? The Jungle House is nestled in the heart of Hengchun Peninsula, embraced by lush greenery. It's a sanctuary designed for those who long for freedom and peace. Whether it's a family reunion or a corporate retreat, we provide the ultimate private space.

We believe the essence of travel lies in "connection" — connecting with nature and meaningful conversations with friends. Our villa blends rustic charm with modern luxury, allowing you to enjoy top-tier facilities while feeling the natural pulse of the earth. We are also a Pet-Friendly B&B, welcoming your furry friends to join the vacation.

Located in the central area of Hengchun, convenience is at your doorstep with supermarkets nearby. Just a 10-minute drive to the azure waters of South Bay, your stay perfectly balances convenience and a dreamlike getaway. We are widely recognized as the best value private villa choice.
